## Vision Statement

> **For** [people who struggle with daily outfit decisions]  
> **Who** [want to dress confidently and appropriately without spending hours planning]  
> **Our product** [WearItRight]  
> **Is a** [web-based smart styling platform]  
> **That** [generates personalized outfit suggestions from a user’s existing wardrobe using weather, occasion, and style preferences]  
> **Unlike** [Pinterest boards or generic fashion apps]  
> **Our product** [is practical, fast, and built around clothes you already own — not pushing you to buy more]
