## Vision Statement

> **For** [people who struggle with daily outfit decisions]  
> **Who** [want to dress confidently and appropriately without spending hours planning]  
> **Our product** [WearItRight]  
> **Is a** [web-based smart styling platform]  
> **That** [generates personalized outfit suggestions from a user’s existing wardrobe using weather, occasion, and style preferences]  
> **Unlike** [Pinterest boards or generic fashion apps]  
> **Our product** [is practical, fast, and built around clothes you already own — not pushing you to buy more]

---

## Target Users

WearItRight serves 3 specific end-user groups:
Busy Students & Young Professionals — “Riya Sen”  
Ages 20-30, low fashion confidence, need 10-second weather-appropriate outfits for class/work.

Fashion Enthusiasts & Content Creators — “Emily Carter”  
Ages 22-35, strong fashion/tech skills, want advanced customization to maximize their existing wardrobe.

Time-Pressed Working Professionals — “Marcus Chen”  
Ages 25-35, intermediate style sense, need fast, weather-smart outfits for meetings without decision fatigue.

Shared need: All own underutilized wardrobes and want to dress confidently in seconds, using clothes they already have.

## User Stories

Format: _As a [persona], I want [goal], so that [benefit]._

| # | Persona | User Story | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
